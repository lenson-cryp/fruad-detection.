Recovery Programs

Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP)

A structured treatment program that allows you to maintain your daily responsibilities while receiving the support you need.

Duration: 8-12 weeks

Schedule: 3-4 sessions per week, 2-3 hours each

Includes: Group therapy, individual counseling, skill-building workshops, and relapse prevention planning

Learn More

Relapse Prevention Program

Specialized support for individuals in early recovery or those who have experienced a recent relapse.

Duration: Ongoing support

Schedule: Weekly sessions

Includes: Trigger identification, coping strategies, lifestyle balance, and crisis planning

Learn More

Mindfulness-Based Recovery

Learn to manage cravings and emotional triggers through evidence-based mindfulness practices.

Duration: 10 weeks

Schedule: Weekly 90-minute sessions

Includes: Meditation training, mindful awareness practices, and integrated recovery support

Learn More
